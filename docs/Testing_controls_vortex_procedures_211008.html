3.2. The vortex step

Purpose: Testing various procedures during the KCl extraction step. Here I test various methods of shaking the Blank + 2M KCl added solution . This is a sanity test whether cross-contamination of samples happen during vortexing and spin-down procedures. Method: Various methods (1) 30s: Vortex with Gini vortexer and spindown 0rpm -> 2000rpm for 30 seconds. (2) 5min: Vortex with Gini vortexer and spindown 4000rpm for 5 min. (this will strongly remove all droplets from the seal, which could be the source of cross-contamination when peeling the seal from the 96 well plate) (3) Shake: Shake with the table top shaker in front of the MilliQ for 30 seconds without seeling. This would not be recommended when using real soils. (4) Pipetting: Use the pipette to homogenize the solution. This would not be used when using real soils. 3 replicates of various nitrate concentrations in 2M KCl was used (0mM to 10mM -> in the final measurements it will be diluted to 1/5)
